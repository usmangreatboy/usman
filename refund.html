Refund

No Refund Policy

Fiberish Telelink Pvt. Ltd. maintains a strict No Refund Policy. Once a payment has been processed for our services, it is non-refundable. This policy applies to all types of payments including, but not limited to, subscription fees, installation charges, and any additional service-related fees.

Why We Have This Policy

Service Commitment: We are dedicated to maintaining and improving our services for the benefit of all customers. The funds collected are immediately allocated towards maintaining our network, supporting infrastructure, and improving service quality.

Operational Costs: Our operational expenses are incurred at the time of service activation and throughout the service period. These costs include infrastructure maintenance, customer support, and other overheads necessary to provide uninterrupted service.

Customer Satisfaction

We value our customers and aim to provide excellent service at all times. If you experience any issues with our service, please contact our customer support team. We are here to help resolve any problems and ensure your satisfaction.
